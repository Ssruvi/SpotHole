Spothole is a platform dedicated to reporting potholes in our community to facilitate their repairs and ensure safer roads for everyone.

To report a problem,simply sign in or log in to your Spothole account and fill out the form provided on our website with the necessary details about the potholes location and severity..
The time it takes to fix a reported pothole can vary depending on various factors such as the severity of the pothole, weather conditions, and the workload of the maintenance crew. We strive to address reported potholes as soon as possible.
No, there is no cost associated with reporting a pothole on our website. It's a free service provided to help maintain the safety of our roads.
